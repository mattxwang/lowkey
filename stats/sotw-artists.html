*An astute observer might note that this is an undercount (with minor overcounts)! Good observation. I list artists generally based off of the Spotify listing when I add the song. Some notes on methodology:

  • Uncredited features are not counted, like Playboi Carti on EARFQUAKE.
  • I subjectively choose when a group versus individual members are credited. For example, I don't count 88rising as an artist, but I do for K/DA; I view 100 gecs as Dylan Brady and Laura Les, not different artists.
  • Producers are inconsistently undercounted. Generally, they're not credited, but they occasionally are. Madlib seems to be credited on all of his beats; Kenny isn't on ARE YOU WITH THAT?, but is on get off my dick. I'll try to explore producers another time!
